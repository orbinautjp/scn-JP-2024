<h1>Lunar transfer</h1>
<p>Two scenarios representing snapshots of a lunar mission.</p>
<p>The first scenario has a Shuttle-A in low Earth orbit, preparing for
trans-lunar injection to set it on a transfer orbit to intercept the Moon.</p>
<p>The second scenario shows the same vessel some time later, after the TLI burn
has completed.</p>
